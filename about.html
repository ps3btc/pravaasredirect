pravaas.org is based on a simple idea: High quality mentor-ship and advice must be available to every student anywhere in this world for free. Join pravaas.org either as a mentor or as a student and make a difference with your time, ideas and advice. Pravaas stands for Journey in sanskrit, so let this just be a pit stop. Post any question you may have in the discuss link above and a mentor will be assigned to you. pravaas.org was founded by Hareesh Nagarajan and Prem Mohanty in early October 2009.

pravaas.org used to be hosted as a social network on ning.com (pravaas.ning.com). In May 2010, ning.com announced they were going pay-only. This aggravated one of the founders and he decided to take matters into his own hands and host the entire network himself. The result is a much simpler and easy to use service. And to top it all, going forward, you do not need to sign up for a ning.com account. To post a message you just need a Google Account! You do not need to give us your password! Sometimes disruptive changes are for the better!

Check out the archives to get a sense of the kinds of questions that have been asked in the past.
